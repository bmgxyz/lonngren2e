\textbf{1.5.1} Express the signal $v(t) = 100\cos{(120\pi t - 45\degree)}$ in phasor notation.

\begin{equation*}
	\begin{split}
		V(\chi) & = 100e^{j(-45\degree)} \\
		& = 100e^{-j\frac{\pi}{4}} \\
		& = 50\sqrt{2}(1 - j)
	\end{split}
\end{equation*}
