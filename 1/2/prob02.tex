\textbf{1.2.2.} Find the vector $\mathbf{B}$ from the origin to the opposite corner that lies in the $xy$ plane.

The vector begins at $(0,0,0)$ and ends at $(a,a,0)$, so \[\mathbf{B} = \begin{pmatrix} a \\ a \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}.\]
